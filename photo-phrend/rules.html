Submission Rules

To submit your photos to the website and compete in our photo contests, you MUST follow the guidelines.

Acceptable Content

  • You must submit your own original content. Photos must be unaltered, other than cropping.
  • Keep it clean.
  • No photos over 1mb (if you need to convert your photo size to be accepted, we suggest using a conversion website such as: freeconvert.com.)
  • File formats accepted are JPEG, PNG, and GIF. (Animated GIFs will not be accepted).
  • No more than 5 entries per person.
  • You can submit any photos that relate to the categories available.

Contest Rules

  • All enteries will be accepted for the monthly contests starting the first of the month, starting at 11:59pm on the day of the prior month.
  • Voting will available after all submissions are made, and will run until the end of the month.
  • All entries must be submitted within the first 2 weeks of the month by 11:59pm.
  • Winners will be announced, awarded and featured in our winners gallery on the first of the month.
