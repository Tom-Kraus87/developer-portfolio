Frequently Asked Questions

What services does TomTech offer?

TomTech provides a wide range of services including IT support, cloud solutions, server management, network design, scripting and automation, technical consulting, cybersecurity, virtualization, and website development/testing.

How much does Remote IT Support cost?

Remote IT Support costs $300 for specific, common fixes, such as system setup and basic troubleshooting.

What is included in the Small Business Startup Package?
  • This package includes:
    • Office 365 & Cloud Solutions Consulting
    • Website Development, Testing & Maintenance
    • Backup & Disaster Recovery Solutions
  • The package is priced at $4,500, which includes a 10% discount on individual services.
Do you provide training for end-users?

Yes, TomTech offers End-User Training & Documentation for $2,500. This service helps improve IT literacy and reduce support costs.

Can I get a discount for bundling services?

Yes, TomTech offers several package deals that provide discounts when you bundle services. For example, the IT Infrastructure Setup & Management Package costs $6,000 and includes a 20% discount on individual services.

What are your prices for website development?

Website Development starts at $1,500 for basic static websites (HTML, CSS, JS, JQuery). More complex dynamic websites may have higher costs.

What is your hourly rate for one-time projects?

Hourly rates are available for one-time projects. Please contact TomTech for more details about pricing and rates.

What is the price for Cybersecurity & Compliance Audits?

Cybersecurity & Compliance Audits start at $3,000, depending on the size of the organization and the complexity of the environment.

Do you offer server setup and management services?

Yes, server setup, configuration, and management are offered at $3,000 for complete setup. Prices may vary depending on the size and complexity of the organization.

Can you help with cloud-based solutions like Office 365?

Yes, TomTech specializes in Office 365 & Cloud Solutions Consulting, starting at $1,000 depending on the number of users and complexity involved.
