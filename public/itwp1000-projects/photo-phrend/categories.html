Categories

While you may have many pictures of various categories that you would love to share and you feel would be worthy of winning a contest, we ask that you submit content related to the categories below. If you have suggestions of other content categories that you would enjoy seeing featured on our website, please feel free to visit our contact page and send us a message.


Before you start submitting your photos and joining our photo club, we strongly suggest visiting our rules page. You don't want to break any rules or submit any photos that aren't allowed or that would be deemed inappropriate by the administrator, otherwise these photos will not be posted. Feel free to navigate the website to familiarize yourself and get a better understanding of what is accepted.

Photo Categories

The categories of photos featured in our gallery and accepted for the contest:

  • Animals (Pets or Wildlife)

    • Here at Photo Phrend, we love animals and would love to see any pictures of animals you'd like to share with us. This could be pictures of your pets, pictures of your friends pets, fish at an aquarium, a squirrel outside, even insects.

  • Nature

    • Nature is always a great subject for photography. You can take beautiful pictures of in all seasons. Examples of nature photos include flowers, trees, lakes, sunsets, and more.

  • Food

    • We all take pictures of our food. Food is anything that anyone can eat or drink. Some examples of our featured food photos are in our gallery. Feel free to take a look and get inspired.
